About Me

I'm the analyst who asks "why" five times and won't stop until complex problems become clear, actionable solutions. My journey into analytics began through entrepreneurship: co-founding a music streaming platform taught me that behind every business decision is a story the data can tell.

I am currently pursuing my M.S. in Business Analytics at the University of South Dakota, where I also serve as President of the USD Business Analytics Club. Under my leadership, the club has grown to over 40 members and partnered with local businesses to develop practical data-driven solutions. In a recent forecasting project, I helped a bakery secure more favorable supplier contracts, allowing them to maintain pricing without passing increased costs to customers.

My unique combination of entrepreneurial experience, technical expertise across SQL, R, Python, visualization tools, and exceptional communication skills allows me to translate complex analytical findings into compelling business narratives. Whether presenting to executives or teaching fellow students, I excel at making the technical accessible and the complex simple.

I'm seeking entry-level business analyst or analytics engineering roles where I can apply my evidence-based approach to solve meaningful problems in supply chain, marketing intelligence, or product analytics—with the ultimate goal of leading analytical teams that drive strategic business decisions.
